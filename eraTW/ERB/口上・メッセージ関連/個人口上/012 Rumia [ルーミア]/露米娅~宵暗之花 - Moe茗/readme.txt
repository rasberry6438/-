【关于口上启用后的修改】

1、为契合口上的设定，去掉[不在乎贞操]，[不知羞恥]，增加[老实]，[开朗]的特质
  请不要自行修改角色特质，以免造成不必要的bug，如有修改造成的存档爆炸的bug，作者不对其负任何责任
  In order to stay consistent with the dialogue , it will remove TALENT:30[Unchaste] and TALENT:33[Shameless] ,and added TALENT:12[Docile] and TALENT:24[Cheerful]
  DON'T modify those character personality by yourself , lest it cause unnecessary bugs.
  If your modification results in the destruction of the saves , the author is not responsible for it.

2、初见之后，将自动读取现在的主人公称呼，作为露米娅独有称呼
  可以在初见之后于update中修改
  After your encounter with Rumia for the firsttime , the system will check the "callname" of your own character , as the unique title of her.
  You can revise the unique title in the "update menu".

3、为契合口上的设定，去掉了露米娅在tw原版中所有的，会在在火曜日时，前往地藏小路的“食料确保”工作
  The original TW Rumia has a job named "Food Gathering" on every Tuesday, it has been removed to stay consistent with the Kojo.

【其他的注意事项】

1.本口上在很多地方，假定主人公是成年正常体型男性人类，所以选择其他体型，以及百合和扶她等，有部分剧情可能会出现逻辑冲突……
 The dialogue may assume the custom character as normal-sized male human in many action , so if your character has different size or gender , it may lead to logical conflicts.  

2.本口上可能包含二设已及部分ooc内容，相比东方project原有设定，可能存在不一致的情况，介意勿用，还请嘴下留情（）
  The dialogue may have some personality which from other Touhou fan works and my imagination , so those may not be consistent with the Touhou original settings.
  I feel sorry if you mind , please show mercy.

3.本口上目前还处于内容不全的现状，如有部分指令毫无口上，是正常现象……
  The dialogue is currently incomplete . So it's normal phenomenon if there are some commands with out any dialogue...

4.本口上的内容均属幻想文学，所涉及角色均已年满18周岁。
  请勿在现实生活中模仿，请勿向未成年人传播其内容，本作者不对任何直接或间接事故负责
  The content of this dialogues all belongs to fantasy literature . All the characters involved are over 18 years old.
  DON'T imitate those in real life。 DON'T share those content to minors. The author is not responsible for any direct or indirect accidents.
